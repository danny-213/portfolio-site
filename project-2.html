DATA ANALYTICS

Often, companies have data that contain a lot of hidden information. Asking the right questions and analyzing the data will give you relevant insights. The goal is to answer the right questions that will help the company to make the right decisions.

Project Overview

Which channels are the most effective? Which area, region has the best average performance? How to optimize stocking plan from sales forecast? When I look further into the ordering process, I found that consumer behvior are strong affect by season, especially by year end November and December and slow down on January (from the chart). Utilizing simple statistical models, moving average, smoothing, we are able to combine the past trends and seasonality.

Project duration varies between 2 weeks and 3 months. In order to carry out the project as quickly as possible, it is important that the relevant data is available, complete and clean.
